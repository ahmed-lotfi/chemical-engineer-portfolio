About Me.

Passionate recent graduate in chemical engineering with a focus on process and operations engineering for the oil, gas, and petrochemical industry. Solid theoretical knowledge in Process Safety Management (PSM) and expertise in diverse process hazard analysis tools (What If, SWIFT, HAZID, HAZOP, Bowtie, etc.).

Well-versed in the application of industry codes, standards, and design practices for Process Equipment Design, including API-14E, API-12J, TEMA, ISA, and adherence to leading companies' design criteria. Aiming for reliable and safe designs to ensure the longevity of plant operations.
